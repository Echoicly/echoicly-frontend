Echoicly – Idea Bank and Core Features

A fair and transparent alternative to today’s music streaming platforms

1. Revenue Distribution

  • Subscription price: 199 NOK/month
  • 10% goes to administration and operations
  • 90% is paid directly to the artists the user listens to
  • Two distribution models:
    • Per song
    • Per minute listened
  • Users choose their preferred model

2. Extra Support to Artists

  • Users can give additional support to artists at any time
  • 100% of extra amounts go directly to the artist – no fees
  • Personal overview of which artists the user has supported

3. Transparency and Reporting

  • Monthly report for each user:
    • Listening patterns and artist payments
    • What the administration share has been used for
  • Visual graphs and pedagogical explanations
  • Full transparency for both users and artists

4. User Control and Democratization

  • Users control how their subscription fee is distributed
  • Option to prioritize favorite artists
  • Insights into the system’s mechanisms and principles

5. Surplus and Community Funds

  • Any surplus (after admin costs) may be:
    • Returned to artists through a fan-guided reward scheme
    • Used for music promotion or support funds
  • Users can vote on how the surplus should be allocated

6. Merch and Direct Sales

  • Artists can sell merch directly through the app
  • No commission taken by Echoicly
  • Purchases are shown as direct support to the artist

7. App and User Experience

  • Modern, clean, and intuitive app design
  • Includes:
    • Artist profiles
    • Playlists and recommendations
    • Recently listened section
    • “My account” with financial breakdown
  • Interactive features for support, merch and customization

8. Artist–Fan Relationships and Rewards

  • Artists can message fans directly in the app (e.g. thanks, previews, announcements)
  • Top listeners receive exclusive benefits:
    • Early access to tickets and merch
    • Special edition content
  • Strengthens loyalty and emotional bond between listener and artist

9. Artist Tools and Insights

  • Artist dashboard includes:
    • Real-time income and listener stats
    • Ability to communicate with fans
    • Upload merch and manage artist page
  • Simple and accessible interface for all artists

10. Community and Sharing

  • Users can:
    • Share playlists and causes
    • Invite friends to support artists together
    • Optionally follow others and see their listening activity

11. Play and Engagement

  • Gamification through badges/points for:
    • Supporting artists
    • Discovering new music
    • Sharing content
  • Weekly challenges or campaigns
  • Collectible fan items (e.g. digital fan badges)

12. Ethical Technology

  • No sale of user data to third parties
  • Users fully own their data – downloadable and deletable
  • Transparent use of all data and revenue models

13. Environment and Sustainability

  • Optional low-res streaming for reduced data use
  • Local caching to minimize bandwidth
  • Climate labeling for eco-aware music projects
  • Portion of surplus can be allocated to green initiatives
  • Green technology and hosting as guiding principles
